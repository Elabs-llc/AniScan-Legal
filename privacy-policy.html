Introduction

Welcome to AniScan, an AI-powered eye condition screening application developed by Elabs LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data.

AniScan is designed as an offline-first application specifically to serve users in underserved areas with limited internet connectivity. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Information We Collect

1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device (Primary Storage)

All core app data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Eye Scan Images: Temporarily stored in RAM during analysis, then automatically deleted. Never saved to disk unless you explicitly export a scan.
  • Scan Results: Analysis results stored in an encrypted local database (Hive).
  • User Preferences: Your app settings including language, offline mode, haptic feedback, and voice guidance.
  • Scan History: Historical record of your scans stored locally.
Important: All this data stays on your device. We do not have access to it, and it is not transmitted anywhere unless you explicitly share it.

2. Data Collected Online (Optional, Opt-In Only)

When you have internet connectivity and have NOT enabled "Offline Mode":

Anonymous Usage Analytics (via Firebase, opt-in)

  • App screens visited
  • Features used
  • Scan frequency (not scan content)
  • Device type and OS version

Crash Reports (via Firebase, opt-in)

  • App crash logs
  • Device state at time of crash
  • No personal information or scan data

You can disable analytics and crash reporting at any time in Settings → Analytics.

3. Data We DO NOT Collect

We never collect:

  • ❌ Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location data or GPS coordinates
  • ❌ Biometric data (beyond temporary scan images)
  • ❌ Medical records or health information
  • ❌ Contact lists or other app data
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Financial information

How We Use Your Information

Primary Use: Local Analysis

Your eye scan images are processed using TensorFlow Lite running entirely on your device. The AI model analyzes the image and provides results without any data leaving your device.

Secondary Use: Cloud Fallback (Optional)

If on-device analysis fails and you're online (with Offline Mode disabled), we may send anonymized eye images to our cloud API. These images are:

  • Processed immediately
  • Never stored on our servers
  • Deleted after analysis completes
  • Not linked to your identity

Tertiary Use: App Improvement (Optional, Opt-In)

If you enable analytics, we use aggregated, anonymized data to:

  • Understand which features are most used
  • Identify bugs and crashes
  • Improve app performance
  • Plan new features

Third-Party Services

1. TensorFlow Lite (Google)

  • Purpose: On-device AI inference
  • Data Sent: None (runs entirely on your device)
  • Privacy Policy: tensorflow.org/lite

2. Google ML Kit

  • Purpose: Face detection and eye detection
  • Data Sent: None (runs entirely on your device)
  • Privacy Policy: developers.google.com/ml-kit

3. Firebase (Google) - Optional, Opt-In

  • Purpose: Analytics and crash reporting
  • Data Sent: Anonymous usage data, crash reports (no personal info)
  • Control: Can be disabled in Settings
  • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Firebase only activates when:

  • ✅ You have internet connectivity
  • ✅ You have NOT enabled "Offline Mode"
  • ✅ You have NOT disabled analytics in Settings

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

Right to Access

  • View all your scan history: Settings → History
  • Export your data as JSON: Settings → Export Data
  • Export scans as PDF: History → Select Scan → Share as PDF

Right to Delete

  • Delete individual scans: History → Select Scan → Delete
  • Delete all scans: Settings → Delete All Scans
  • Uninstall app to remove all local data

Right to Control

  • Enable/Disable Offline Mode: Settings → Offline Mode
  • Enable/Disable Analytics: Settings → Analytics
  • Enable/Disable Voice Guidance: Settings → Voice Guidance
  • Enable/Disable Haptic Feedback: Settings → Haptic Feedback

Right to Data Portability

  • Export all data: Settings → Export Data (JSON format)
  • Share individual scans: History → Select Scan → Share as PDF

Children's Privacy

AniScan is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Parental consent is required for users between 13-18 years of age.

Offline Mode

Offline Mode is our recommended setting for maximum privacy.

When Offline Mode is enabled:

  • ✅ All AI analysis happens on your device
  • ✅ No data sent to any servers
  • ✅ Firebase analytics disabled
  • ✅ No internet connection required
  • ✅ 100% private and secure

To enable: Settings → Offline Mode → ON
