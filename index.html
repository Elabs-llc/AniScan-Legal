Why Choose AniScan?

A mobile application that uses artificial intelligence to help screen for common eye conditions including cataracts and glaucoma. Designed specifically for users in underserved areas with limited internet connectivity, AniScan works 100% offline to bring accessible eye care screening to everyone.
